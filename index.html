Authentic, Relevant Technology Integration through Networking

Join teachers from across San Diego County to discuss all things Ed Tech! These online gatherings are an opportunity to exchange ideas, gather resources and support each other, along with have a few laughs as we share our stories of triumphs and challenges. The Educational Technology team at the San Diego County Office of Education supports these gatherings by hosting the safe space, linking up educators to present relevant practices for current challenges, and connecting with students around the county. Walk away with new ideas, resources, energy, and possibly new gear! We will email reminders and zoom links the week before each meeting. Use the yellow registration button located above for the most current meeting, and also located next to the meeting descriptions below. 

2021-22 Network Meeting Descriptions

Supporting all Students - A Response to the Main Take-Aways from our Student Panel
Monday, October 18, 2021 @ 4-5 p.m.

We just heard from students and we listened! Three main themes came out of the student panel we held last month. The first, that one:one support is a highly sought after strategy for student success, specifically via online office hours. The second, from our elementary students, that online games are engaging and a fun way to practice the skills being taught. And lastly, that digital assignments where student creativity was a key to showcasing new or strengthened knowledge helped to make the learning go deeper.
Join the next EdTech Network to hear from teachers about how they are engaging students through these three strategies.

We are a collaborative team focused on accelerating, scaling, and amplifying the number and speed of innovations coming from the San Diego County Office of Education (SDCOE). We provide support to district and school leaders, staff, students, and the greater San Diego community to translate ideas into impact and generate new solutions at every level.
